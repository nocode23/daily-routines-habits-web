Your day at a glance

All your routines grouped by area of life. See exactly what's done, what's left, and how far you've come today. Tap an area header to collapse it — sections open up again whenever you switch days.

Three meaningful states

Every routine is clearly incomplete, completed (gently dimmed), or archived (greyed out). No confusion about what needs your attention.

30 days of progress

Track your completion rate, current and longest streaks, and see your habits on a GitHub-style heatmap. Spot patterns. Stay consistent.

Routines that fit your life

Set routines for every day or specific weekdays. Choose morning, afternoon, evening — or repeat throughout the whole day. Set a goal count (e.g. 8 glasses of water) or just track completion.

Reminders that actually help

Set notifications at specific times or automatically every N hours. The app respects your iOS notification budget and shows exactly when reminders fire.

Check off from your Home Screen

Add a widget in small, medium, or large size — or on your Lock Screen. Tap to toggle a routine without even opening the app. Always shows today's progress and starts fresh every morning.
